About Us

Clemwolly provides Comprehensive Logistics Solutions: tech-enabled delivery services that allow enterprises to evolve, optimize and prepare networks for long-term strategic growth. A single technology integration opens access to nationwide delivery operations across Nigeria.

Founded in 2010 and headquartered in Lagos, Clemwolly brings deep logistics expertise and enterprise-grade technology to deliver innovative e-Commerce fulfillment, retail distribution and network capacity solutions to Fortune 500 enterprises.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping usually take?

Shipping times vary depending on the destination. Domestic deliveries typically take 1-3 business days, while international shipments can take 3-10 business days.

Do you offer package tracking?

Yes, we provide real-time tracking for all shipments. You'll receive a tracking number once your package is dispatched.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please contact our customer service for specific details about your shipment.

How do I get a quote for my shipment?

You can get a quote by filling out our online form or by contacting our customer service team directly.
